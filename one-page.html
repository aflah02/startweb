I am the son of a retired Air Commodore from the Indian Air Force. I have a total of 27 years of corporate experience in various MNC's. A seasoned professional with deep expertise in the field of sales and marketing, my 27-year-old career spans several industries. From telecom to travel, wealth management and lately as a wellness coach, I have been recognized as a visionary thinker, strategist and planner.
